Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TechFest

What is the registration fee? +

Registration is completely free for all students. We believe in making technology accessible to everyone. Simply register on our website and you're all set!

Can I participate in multiple events? +

Absolutely! You can register for as many events as you want. However, make sure to check the event schedules to avoid conflicts.

Do I need experience to participate? +

No experience required! We have events for beginners, intermediate, and advanced participants. Choose events that match your skill level.

Will there be accommodations? +

Yes, accommodations are available for students from out of station. We'll provide details on our accommodation page after registration.

How do I register for events? +

Simply click the "Register Now" button on our website. Fill in your details, select your events, and you're done! You'll receive confirmation email immediately.

What if I can't attend? +

If you need to cancel, contact us at least 7 days before the event. We'll be happy to help or transfer your registration to a friend.
